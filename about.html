Milestones

1973

Headquartered in Kuwait, United Realty Company established by an Amiri Decree as a licensed public Kuwaiti Shareholding Company.

1975

URC’s portfolio comprises 15 projects in Kuwait, and assets in Boston, USA.

1976

Develops Bibi Residential Complex in Dai’ya, Kuwait.

1978

Develops Al Maseel Complex, a mixeduse property in Kuwait City.

1979

Develops Al Mutahida Complex, a mixeduse property in Kuwait City.

1983

Development of landmark mixeduse property, Marina World, in Salmiya and Saleh Shehab Resort in Julia’a, Kuwait.

1984

United Realty Company rebrands to United Real Estate Company.

1985

Acquires City Tower office and commercial building in Kuwait City.
